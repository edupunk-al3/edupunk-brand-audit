## What Is This?

Most people carry a brand inside long before they can name it. This framework makes it sharp.

Through a 3-30 hour guided interview with an AI (Claude recommended, Opus 4.6 model), you'll excavate your brand's DNA across **108 structured questions** and receive two deliverables:

1. **Raw Interview Data** — a complete, unedited transcript preserving every question, answer, hesitation, and correction. Your source of truth.
2. **Brand Context Profile** — a structured, AI-readable reference document that serves as the "context base" for all future brand-related conversations.

## Who Is This For?

- Anyone building a personal brand
- Brand stewards managing an organization's identity
- Founders whose brand is evolving beyond themselves
- Anyone curious about deep self-exploration through the lens of brand

## Quick Start

1. Open [Claude](https://claude.ai) (Opus 4.6 recommended)
2. Copy the contents of [`SKILL.md`](./SKILL.md)
3. Paste it into your conversation
4. Begin your journey

That's it. The framework handles the rest — calibration, question flow, archetype analysis, session management, and document generation.

## Framework Overview

### Calibration

Before the 108 questions begin, the framework calibrates your relationship with your brand:

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **A — Personal Brand** | The brand IS you |
| **B — Brand Steward** | You lead a brand larger than yourself |
| **C — Founder in Transition** | The brand grew from you but is becoming its own entity |

### The Three Journeys

**Journey 1: North Star & Identity** (Q1-Q18)
- Who you admire and why — traced to 3rd-order influences
- Your industry, your gap, your Ikigai intersection
- First archetype hypothesis

**Journey 2: Meaning Engineering** (Q19-Q73)
- Beliefs & contrarian takes
- Expression mechanics
- Aesthetic crimes
- Voice & personality
- Second archetype calibration

**Journey 3: Brand Practice** (Q74-Q108)
- Content architecture & rhythm
- Hard Nos — lines you won't cross
- Red flags — how you spot the fake
- Final archetype confirmation

### Archetype System

The framework uses the Brand Twelve Archetypes (rooted in Jungian theory, developed by Margaret Mark & Carol S. Pearson) as an analytical undercurrent. Three **Archetype Anchor Points** throughout the interview progressively refine your archetype profile.

## Design Principles

Three convictions that shaped this framework:

1. **Context assets must be stored locally** — not locked in any single model's cloud. They should be portable across platforms.
2. **If AI is your scaffolding, it must deliver 10x better outcomes** — and translate into real-world action.
3. **Structures created with AI should be open-sourced** — this is not individual intelligence. Don't hoard it.
